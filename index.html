Average shots per minute
To find this number: set a timer for 2 minutes and count every shot you attempt. Do not worry about makes or misses. Repeat 4–5 times. Add your shot totals together, divide by the number of rounds to get your average shots in 2 minutes, then divide that number by 2. Enter that final number here.

Example: 30 + 32 + 28 + 30 = 120 shots. 120 ÷ 4 rounds = 30 shots in 2 minutes. 30 ÷ 2 = 15 shots per minute.
